Capstone Project Information

Level 4 students collaborated to design and develop a website for the Brothers In Arms Memorial, a tribute by the Srishti Foundation dedicated to honoring Indian and Canadian military veterans in London, ON.

Explore projects from our IDP3 and UED students, featuring Spotlight presentations that highlight the impactful work behind the Brothers In Arms initiative.

Program Information

The Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College is a two-year journey into the world of digital creativity, blending design, development, and interactive storytelling. Whether you're passionate about UX/UI, web development, animation, or multimedia production, this program provides a hands-on learning experience that prepares you for a dynamic career in the industry.

Graduates of the Interactive Media Design program are well-equipped for careers in web design, front-end development, digital marketing, graphic design, and beyond. With a strong portfolio of real-world projects, students enter the workforce ready to make an impact!
