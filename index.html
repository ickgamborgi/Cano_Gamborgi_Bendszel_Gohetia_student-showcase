Capstone Project Information

Level 4 students took on the challenge of designing and developing a website for the Brothers In Arms Memorial, an initiative by the Srishti Foundation dedicated to honoring military veterans.

Join us for a showcase featuring the work of our IDP3 and UED students, alongside Spotlight presentations highlighting the The Brothers in Arms project.

Experience the talent and dedication of our students as they proudly present their portfolios, showcasing their passion and skills across various disciplines.
